Join Us at IROS 2024

Abu Dhabi

We are excited to announce that Intrepid AI will be participating in the tutorial session at the 2024 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS 2024). This prestigious conference will be held from October 14 – 18, 2024 at ADNEC in Abu Dhabi, UAE.

About IROS 2024

Workshops & Tutorials

Intrepid AI at IROS 2024

IROS is one of the largest and most important robotics research conferences in the world. It provides a platform for the international robotics community to exchange knowledge and ideas about the latest advances in intelligent robots and smart machines. The theme of IROS 2024 is “Robotics for Sustainable Development”, focusing on the role of robotics in achieving sustainability goals.
