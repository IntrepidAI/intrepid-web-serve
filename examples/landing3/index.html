FAQ

Answers to all your questions

How does Intrepid work?

Intrepid AI provides a visual tool to design robotics behaviors and a simulator to test and validate them. You can use, mix&match our pre-built capabilities or build your own. Once you are done, you can simulate them and test their robustness.

What skills are required to benefit from Intrepid AI?

Intrepid AI allows you to create robotics behaviors with the visual tools we provide, for which no code needs to be written. However, you can seamlessly integrate you Python, C/C++ or Rust code using our SDK. We plan on adding more languages in the future too.

Can I integrate Intrepid AI with my existing codebase?

Absolutely! We not only allow you to integrate your existing libraries and standalone algorithms. You can also integrate your ROS2 infrastructure via topics, actions and services.

Can I use Intrepid AI with my team mates?

Yes you can! The dashboard offers a collaborative editor that you can use to build robotics behaviors together with your team members or with any other external collaborator. It is as easy as sharing a link.

Is Intrepid more suitable for beginners or expert roboticists?

While expert roboticists will benefit from the Intrepid platform and speed up development time, Intrepid AI is also beneficial to less expert developers who can reuse our pre-built components and build effective robotics capabilities with little to no code.
